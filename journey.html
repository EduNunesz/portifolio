JOURNEY

Metascore: 92

Release: 2015

Platforms: PS3, PS4 and PC

In Journey, the player controls a hooded figure wandering through a vast desert, traveling towards a large mountain in the distance. There is the possibility of finding another player during the exploration of the scenarios and forming a mutual aid partnership with him in the middle of the journey, however, there is no communication via speech or text and no information is provided regarding the other user.

Developed by: Eduardo Nunes
