FIREWATCH

Metascore: 76

Release: 2016

Platforms: PC, Xbox One, PS4 and Switch

The story follows a forest ranger named Henry in the Shoshone National Forest, one year after the 1988 Yellowstone wildfires. A month after his first day on the job, strange things start to happen to him and his supervisor Delilah, which connects to a conspiracy mystery that happened years ago. Henry interacts with Delilah using a walkie-talkie, with the player choosing from dialogue options to communicate.

Developed by: Eduardo Nunes
