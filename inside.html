INSIDE

Metascore: 91

Release: 2016

Platforms: PC, Xbox One, PS4 and Switch

About: Inside tells the story of a young boy as he struggles to survive against the evil forces that are trying to take over the world by experimenting on human bodies in a totally questionable way. The boy begins his journey in a forest where strange shadows chase him. After losing them, the boy finds a warehouse, where he sees an entity doing mind control experiments on people.

Developed by: Eduardo Nunes
