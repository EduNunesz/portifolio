CUPHEAD

Metascore: 88

Release: 2017

Platforms: Xbox One, PC and Switch

Cuphead features two players who control the title character and his brother Mugman, on an adventure across the fictional Inkwell Isle to defeat a series of bosses to pay off a debt owed to the devil. The game was praised for its art style, gameplay, soundtrack, and sound difficulty. It was a critical and commercial success, winning several awards and selling over six million copies within three years.

Developed by: Eduardo Nunes
