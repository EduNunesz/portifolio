DISCO ELYSIUM

Metascore: 91

Release: 2019

Platforms: PC, Xbox One, PS4 and Switch

Disco Elysium takes place in the fictional town of Revachol, more specifically in the coastal district of Martinaise, full of crime, poverty and corruption, controlled by the corrupt Longshoremen's Union. Revachol is still scarred by a communist revolution that took place nearly 50 years before the game begins, which overthrew Revachol's former monarchy, which once made Revachol a superpower.

Developed by: Eduardo Nunes
