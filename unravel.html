UNRAVEL

Metascore: 78

Release: 2016

Platforms: PS4, Xbox One and PC

During the game's intro, an elderly woman looks out her window, before adjusting a photo of a baby and grabbing a ball of yarn. As you go up the stairs, a small ball of yarn starts to roll. Yarny, a small anthropomorphic creature made of red wool and the game's protagonist, walks into the scene, looking around at the scenery he is in. According to Sahlin, the wool from which Yarny is made represents love, and that it unfolds as it moves away from those it loves. The description on the official website reads, "Along the way, Yarny unlocks a heartfelt story, bringing together a family's loved ones. Told without a word, Yarny is the tie that ties it all together again.

Developed by: Eduardo Nunes
