UNDERTALE

Metascore: 98

Release: 2015

Platforms: Switch, PS4, PS Vita and PC

Undertale is an electronic role-playing game created by American independent developer Toby Fox. In it, the player can control a human child who has fallen into a cave, a large and isolated region under the surface of the Earth, separated by a magical barrier. Various monsters can be encountered during your quest to return to the surface, primarily through the combat system; the player navigates through the opponent's bullet hell attacks and can choose to pacify or subdue the monsters to spare them instead of murdering them. These choices cause dialogue, characters, and story changes based on the results.

Developed by: Eduardo Nunes
